What's Qwerk?

Qwerk is a secure web-based app that offers chat, cloud-based file sharing and project management tools all rolled into one. This allows workplaces of all shapes and sizes to communicate, collaborate and coordinate efficiently to get things done. To put it simply, it's the smarter way to work.

Our
Qwerk Ethic

Our philosophy is simple, if everyone in an organization works smarter then everything will be done better and faster. That's why we created Qwerk! It's designed to adapt to many quirks of different kinds of teams and different kinds of people. Qwerk helps connect brilliant minds in an easy and intuitive way that allows them to get things done smarter.

Qwerk perks

All communications
in one place

Qwerk is the only app you need to effectively communicate with your team and your clients. Chat with them directly through private messages or set up a group discussion using team channels. It even allows you to save or bookmark conversations for easy access and to customise your notification settings to help you prioritise your projects.

Qwerk features:

Direct Messaging Team Channels Client Chat Star and Pin Messages for Easy Access Customize Notifications to Prioritize Projects

Every tool you need to
manage projects
in one app

No more switching between different apps, Qwerk has built in every single project management tool you will need to track, monitor, and coordinate all your team's projects. You can track your tasks, log activities, add notes and comments as well as record your time to ensure that your projects are running smoothly. You can even get a copy of a detailed task report with a single click of a button.

Project management features:

All Project Managements Tools In One App Task Monitoring Task Logs and Timers One-Click Project Reports Add Notes and Comments

Simplified
File sharing

Drag and drop any type of file into Qwerk for easy sharing and uploading. You can even archive inactive files for safekeeping in our highly secure servers. Rest assured that all internal files and communications uploaded on the app will remain strictly private and secure.

File sharing features:

Drag and Drop Feature Support All File Types Secure Servers Archive Inactive Projects

Access all your
files instantly

Access all the files and conversations you need, when you need them with Qwerk! Whether you're at your desk or on the go, Qwerk's cloud-based system will help you get things done.

Instant access features:

All Content Searchable via Search Bar Cloud-Based File Sharing System Accessible Anywhere and On Any Device
